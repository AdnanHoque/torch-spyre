## Reading guide

  H1 (single-chain) confirmed if: only (1, 1, 32) is < 60 ms
                                  and (m, 1, k) for m>1 are catastrophic
  H2 (n=1 trigger)  confirmed if: all (m, 1, k) walls are < 60 ms
                                  and only the n>1 control is catastrophic
  Mixed: kernel template behaviour depends on more than n alone
