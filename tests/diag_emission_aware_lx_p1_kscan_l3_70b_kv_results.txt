## Reading guide

M1 (chain-coop LX) signature:
  - wall(kf) approximately FLAT across k once overage < 1.0
  - wall(kf) climbs ABOVE that line where overage > 1.0
  - the inflection point predicts k-threshold for chain-LX

M2 (kernel fast path) signature:
  - wall(kf) FLAT across all k regardless of overage
  - id − kf gap is the *only* mode-dependent term

M3 (PSUM forward-pipeline) signature:
  - wall(kf) INCREASES at high k due to growing PSUM payload
  - id − kf gap GROWS with k (more hops × larger payload)
