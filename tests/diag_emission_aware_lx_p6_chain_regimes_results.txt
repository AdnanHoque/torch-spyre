## Reading guide

Compare the regime cost across chain lengths within each shape.
If the (chain ≤ 4) → (chain 8, 16) jump is consistent across
shapes, the regime boundary is universal.
If different shapes have different boundaries, the trigger
depends on something shape-specific (M_per × N? K_per? both?).

If chain=32 'allreduce' regime cost is smaller than chain=8/16:
  the kernel template uses a different reduction primitive at
  chain length = max_cores. Worth understanding architecturally.
