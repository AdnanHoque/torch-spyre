## Reading guide

  - HMI bytes model: which of full-broadcast vs per-cluster
    matches kf walls more closely. If per-cluster wins, the cost
    model needs `bytes /= k` for k>1.
  - PSUM cost: id − kf gives empirical PSUM ms. Compare to model's
    1-hop and m·n-hop predictions. If empirical lies between, the
    hops formula is wrong; if either matches, refine that branch.
