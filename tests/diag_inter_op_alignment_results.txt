## Reading guide

  - 'Extra' = chained wall minus solo walls. Captures inter-op cost.
  - If extra is roughly constant across op2 splits: alignment doesn't matter.
  - If extra is much LOWER for matched (8,1,4): alignment is a lever.
  - 'extra' may be negative if chained pipelines (overlap of op1 and op2)
    — that's fine, what matters is variance across splits.
